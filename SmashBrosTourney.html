Smash Bros Tournament at Bates West

A Smash Bros tournament will be held on [DATE] at Bates West. It will be a competition of only the highest caliber. The battle will be arduous and difficult like childbirth or putting in contacts. Controllers

Controllers will be provided. If you are bipolar, in anger management, or abnormally strong please bring your own controllers. Provided controllers have a turbo button (presses one button automatically) if it starts happening the light on the controller will blink really fast. If this happens it will RUIN you, so just pause and someone will help you fix it.

Rule Set + Poverty Strikes Again

The tournament will be 3-stock (gamer speak for lives) with a time limit of 5:00 minutes. We will use the in-game tournament feature so you will unfortunately only get one fight if you lose. If more than 16 people show up we’ll probably end up taking the amount of people and dividing it by two and having the winners of the two tournaments face off against each other. I also do not have any DLC characters (they cost more than the video game)
